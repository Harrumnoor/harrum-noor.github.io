About Me

Hello! I’m Noor, a graduate student researcher at the University of Waterloo. My focus is on Natural Language to SQL (NL2SQL) translation using Large Language Models. I am passionate about developing technology that bridges human language with data analytics.

Research Focus

Currently, my research focuses on model-free reinforcement learning for dynamic portfolio optimization and evaluating NL2SQL translations using BERT. I’m exploring how BERT-based models enhance natural language interactions with databases.
