About Me

Hello! I’m Noor, a graduate student researcher specializing in Natural Language to SQL (NL2SQL) translation using Large Language Models (LLMs). Currently, I’m pursuing my graduate studies in Computer Science at the University of Waterloo, with a focus on data and machine learning applications.

Research Focus

My research centers on model-free reinforcement learning for dynamic portfolio optimization, and NL2SQL translation using BERT. I’m particularly interested in evaluating NL2SQL translations, improving the accuracy of these models, and exploring how BERT-based models can enhance natural language interactions with databases.

  • Currently experimenting with data augmentation techniques in NL2SQL and working on building a more holistic dataset.
